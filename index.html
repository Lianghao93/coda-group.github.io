Computational Optimization and Data Analytics (CODA) Group

Computational Optimization and Data Analytics (CODA) Group is founded by Dr. Ke Li at University of Exeter in the early 2017. Our research mainly focuses on the computational intelligence, particularly, evolutionary computation (evolutionary multi-objective optimization and applications in industry) and machine learning. Currently, I especially interest in the following topics:

  • Evolutionary multiobjective optimization

  • Large scale optimization

  • Search based software engineering

  • statistical machine learning
